## About Newral

Newral is a digital product engineering and AI solutions studio focused on building scalable, high-performance software systems.

We design, develop, and optimize production-ready platforms including SaaS applications, web platforms, mobile apps, and cloud-native systems.

Newral works with startups and growing companies to build systems that are:

- Scalable
- Performance-driven
- Cost-efficient
- Secure
- Built for real-world usage

Newral operates as a long-term engineering partner, offering both project-based development and ongoing retainer support.
